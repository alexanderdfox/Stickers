💡 Tips

• Rainbow & Sparkle work with ALL tools!

• Use layers to organize complex drawings

• Desktop: Double-click layer name to rename

• Mobile: Tap ✏️ button or long-press layer name to rename

• Right-click (or long-press on mobile) canvas for quick menu

• Copy/Cut/Paste work on active layer

• All shapes support patterns & effects

• Zoom in (+) to see details, zoom out (-) for overview

• Choose canvas size before starting (or change anytime)

• Export at 2× or 4× for high-resolution prints

• Canvas: 800×600 default, up to 1920×1080 Full HD
